The Best Movies According to Andrew

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Man From Earth

I love this movie because it all takes place in one cabin, meaning the focus has to be on the dialogue and the story.

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

Such a trip down nostalgia lane, this movie makes me feel like a kid which is why it's on my list.

Chef

Seeing how passionate the main character is about cooking inspires me and makes me want to do better in my own craft.
